What is an IoT Device URL?

The IoT Device URL is the network address where your Raspberry Pi or IoT device is running. It's used to send automated feed dispensing commands to your device.

How to find your device URL:
  1. Note your Raspberry Pi's IP address or hostname
  2. Make sure the IoT app is running on port 5000
  3. Format: http://[IP_OR_HOSTNAME]:5000
  4. Test by visiting the URL in your browser
Example setup:
Raspberry Pi IP: 192.168.1.100
Device URL: http://192.168.1.100:5000
